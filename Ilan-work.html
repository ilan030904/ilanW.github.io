Welcome to my work! This page showcases a collection of projects I’ve created in my free time, as well as pieces that never made it beyond my computer. I hope you enjoy exploring the creativity and effort behind each project.

This ad concept for Samba Fizz features a bold yellow background and a yellow-tinted can with condensation, highlighting the pineapple juice flavor. The design creates a refreshing, tropical feel that reflects the drink’s vibrant and fruity energy.
The Samba Fizz logo is inspired by the colors of Brazil, reflecting the drink’s Brazilian-themed tropical fruits like pineapple. The sun element adds to the vibrant, refreshing feel, capturing the essence of bold flavor and summer energy.
I designed this graphic using a silhouette of America with flames beneath it and shredded pieces of the Constitution, including phrases like “We the People.” It symbolizes the state of the U.S. in 2025 and was created in response to the White House’s handling of the 2025 LA protests.
This IFC Flag Football flyer is inspired by the 2025 Super Bowl stadium in New Orleans, reimagined with a Pittsburgh twist. Each jersey is customized to match Greek life colors, while the stadium proudly displays “H2P” alongside the Pittsburgh skyline, featuring the city’s iconic bridges and UPMC Tower; blending school spirit with fraternity pride.
