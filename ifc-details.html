Defining the IFC - Fall 2025 Campaign

The campaign served as an introduction to the IFC, clearly defining our organization and its purpose for both current and prospective fraternity members. It features a concise and visually engaging 3-slide post, using varied shapes and formats to highlight key points while maintaining aesthetic appeal. Each slide includes photos of our fraternity members and our University, with a subtle tint applied to ensure the text is easy to read. Through this campaign, we crafted a mission statement aimed at our student body, emphasizing academic excellence and leadership development, while clearly communicating the core values of the IFC. The campaign successfully increased awareness of the IFC, engaged students across campus, and earned recognition from other fraternity accounts nationwide.
